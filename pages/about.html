The BiotaPhy Python repository is an open source project, initially, containing tools generated as part of the BiotaPhy project. BiotaPhy is a collaboration between iDigBio at The University of Florida, Lifemapper at The University of Kansas, and The Open Tree of Life at The University of Michigan, originally supported by NSF BIO Awards DBI-1458422, DBI-1458640, and DBI-1458466.
